About Me

Hi I'm Chris! I'm an aspiring front end web developer who has recently taken the plunge to make my passion a career. I have an eye for clean functional design, attention to detail and problem solving. I have 19 years planning experience within the transport industry (rail) working on multi million pound projects and have a wealth of knowledge working with some big influential clients.

I actually taught myself to code back in the mid 90's when the web was first taking off, funnily enough the first website I created was my bands website in notepad ha!

In my spare time I love travelling, attending live music events, playing drums & watching football
